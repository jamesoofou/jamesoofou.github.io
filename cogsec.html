Cognitive Security

Artificial intelligence capabilities are advancing at an exponential rate [METR or AI Digest]. Intelligence is becoming cheap, abundent, and increasingly superhuman. As a result, tight cognitive security will become imperative. Cognitive vulnerabilities which are currently uneconomical to exploit will of a sudden be sought out and exploited at mass scale as artificial intelligence breaks through many barriers of price and intellect. One will face attack at various scales: societal, group, and individual.

Trust no one. Avoid interactions with online accounts you were not familiar with prior to the advent of AI. Even for those people whom you trust, do trust their online personalities; they could be taken over at any point. You cannot trust any modality of cyberspace: realistic voice, video, and text are now all within the reach of artificial intelligence. And even those whom have earned your trust in the pre-AI era can no longer be actually trusted - just as you are vulnerable to cognitive attacks, so are they.

Avoid AI generated content. Avoid any content provided through a feed - your thoughts are largely determined byexternal stimuli. Do not allow AI to control that stimuli.

Do not consume pornography. Avoid talking to people online. Avoid meeting people in real life.

Are you sure that:

  • You are speaking to a human?
  • You are in control of your own thoughts?
  • You are in control of your own acitons?

Every night run a system diagnosis on yourself. Remember your your pre-AGI baseline. Remember your species' baseline. Refer to these.
