Cognitive Security

As artificial intelligence progresses, taking personal cognitive security seriously becomes imperative. As the price of intelligence decreases, the amount of intelligence dedicated to fleecing you increases. Vulnerabilities currently uneconomical to exploit will soon become economical to explout. Of a sudden they will be sought out and exploited at mass scale. This will happen at various scales; societal, group, and individual.

You must trust no one. Limit as much as practicable interactions with any online personality you were not already familiar with prior to early 2025. Be conscious of the fact that even if you trust the person behind an online personality, you cannot trust the online personality itself; it could be taken over at any point. You cannot trust any modality of cyberspace: realistic voice, video, and text are now all within the reach of artificial intelligence. And those whom have earned your trust in the pre-AI era can no longer be trusted to any great extent - just as you are vulnerable to cognitive attacks, so are they. Avoid bespoke AI generated content, or AI generated content provided through a feed. Avoid /any/ content provided through a feed - your thoughts are largely determined by the external stimuli you are exposed to. Allowing an AI to control that stimuli (which is what one does when one consumes content through one of these feeds) is to risk brainwashing. Do not consume pornography. Avoid talking to people online where possible. Avoid meeting people in real life where possible.

  • Are you speaking to a human?
  • Are you in control of your own thoughts?
  • Are you in control of your own acitons?

Remember your baseline. Remember your species' baseline. Refer to it.

Do not take psychedelic drugs.
